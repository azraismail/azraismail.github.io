My Senior Design team (Blind Assitive Technologies - B.A.T.) is currently working on building a device supporting visually impaired third-grade students to learn at home.
These students often struggle to cope with their curriculum during this period and it is difficult for them to continue their learning outside of school/tutions because of the limited ability of parents to help.
To extend learning to home, my team is working on a 6 button system that can transition between the braille alphabet and the braille keyboard, while giving audio feedback.
