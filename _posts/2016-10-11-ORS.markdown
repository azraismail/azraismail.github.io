I am working as part of the COSMOS initiative - COmputational Skins for Multi-functional Objects and Systems, that aims to develop a skin that can be layered on top of everyday objects to provide new interactions. This involves experiments and measurements with low-cost triboelectric nanogenerators to determine applications and ideal structures to harness energy for novel interactions.

Advised by: Dr. Gregory Abowd
