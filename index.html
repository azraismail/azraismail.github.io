About me

I am an undergraduate student pursuing computer engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am passionate about work that pushes the boundaries of human sensory experiences and bridges the gap between the physical and digital world - making interactions with the cyber world more intuitive. I work with embedded systems, signal processing, and machine learning to engineer such experiences with a strong focus on human-centered design. My research interests lie at the intersection of global health and technology design. My current focus is on using data analytics to assist health providers in low-resource environments in providing relevant health interventions.
